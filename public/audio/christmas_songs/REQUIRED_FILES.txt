HOW TO OBTAIN AUDIO FILES:
---------------------------

Option 1: Royalty-Free Sources
- Freesound.org (search for "christmas instrumental")
- Incompetech.com (Kevin MacLeod - check licensing)
- YouTube Audio Library (free music library)
- Pixabay Music (royalty-free)

Option 2: Create Your Own
- Use instrumental versions of Christmas songs
- Extract 5-10 second snippets from recognizable parts
- Use audio editing software (Audacity, GarageBand, etc.)
- Ensure no vocals (instrumental only)

Option 3: Use AI-Generated Music
- Use AI music generators to create instrumental snippets
- Ensure they're recognizable as the intended songs

IMPORTANT LEGAL NOTES:
----------------------
- Ensure you have proper licensing for any audio files you use
- For commercial use, you may need to purchase licenses
- Public domain songs (like traditional carols) are generally safe
- Modern songs (like "All I Want for Christmas Is You") require licensing

AUDIO SPECIFICATIONS:
---------------------
- Format: MP3 or OGG (OGG preferred for web)
- Length: 5-10 seconds
- Type: Instrumental only (no vocals)
- Volume: Normalized, subtle (not too loud)
- Quality: 128kbps minimum, 192kbps recommended
- Sample Rate: 44.1kHz

PLACEMENT:
----------
Place all files directly in: public/audio/christmas_songs/

The game will work without audio files, but players won't hear the snippets.
The TV will show a music note icon and replay button regardless.
